KYP Kya Hai?

Kushal Yuva Program
Bihar Sarkar ki Yojana

Kushal Yuva Program (KYP) Bihar Sarkar ki ek free skill development yojana hai jo 15 se 25 saal ke yuvaon ke liye hai. Is program mein computer, communication aur soft skills sikhaye jaate hain taki aap job ke liye taiyar ho sako.

KBS Academy Gopalganj ka ek authorized KYP training center hai jahan expert teachers ke saath aap yeh course poora kar sakte hain aur Bihar Skill Development Mission (BSDM) ka official certificate pa sakte hain.
